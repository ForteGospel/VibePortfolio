// about

Dual Boot.

I've spent over a decade as a cybersecurity professional — building SIEM infrastructure, hunting threats, and keeping critical systems safe at Payoneer and Isracard, and before that in the Israeli Air Force's Ofek unit.

But the other side of my brain has always belonged to games. Self-taught in Unity during my CS degree, I've built rhythm games, VR shooters, and narrative adventures. Now I'm formalizing that passion with a Master's in Game Design at Shenkar.

The intersection of both worlds — systems thinking, logic, player behavior, challenge design — is where I do my best work.
